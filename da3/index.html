The Last Laundromat

  • Originality: The originality of my game is the aspect of shooting cats from a cannon and moving throughout the city.
  • Technical Merit: I used a object-oriented approach to phaser with the seperation of scenes and objects. I used a tilemap with varrying layers and tilesets for this assignment instead of a generic background image. I also made my own AI and implemented a healthbar class, this healthbar is interactive with a health system I made for each of the objects (player/ghost). I also had to go into a pre-built plugin and modify it to work with Phaser3 in scene decomposition (WeaponsPlugin.js).
  • Prototype Postmortem: This assignment taught me how to work with more advanced tilemaps with varrying layers and tilesets. The easiest part was getting the previous starter code I had up, running, and tweaked for this new project. The hardest part was debugging an outdated, not updated plugin called WeaponsPlugin.js.

  • High Concept: You are a dude that just got done with his laundry! But the evil ghosts have invaded from the underworld! You must now fight with your cat shooting gun to save the town!
  • Theme: The dead should stay dead. (Spooky ghosts)
  • Mandated Variety: I used keyboard inputs, deterministic gameplay, the achiever, and a shooter game genre.
  • Prototype Goal: The game mechanic being prototyped was the healthbar mechanic and the weapons plugin.
  • Player Experience Goals: The player should feel determination in his goal to purge the world of ghosts.
  • Gameplay: The player can move left, right, jump up, shoot cats, and sprint. When a player shoots a ghost the ghost should take damage with the healthbar decreasing, when the player gets hit by a ghost he should take damage with the healthbar decreasing.
  • Strategies: A good strategy for this is to not rush up the map and to take your time with your hops. You can often times rush up into a fart cloud and die.
  • Story/Setting/Premise: Your name is Dave and you were traumatized by a ghost when you were a little child, now that the ghosts are invading from the underworld you see this as your chance to get back at them while saving the world.
  • Target Audience: The target-audience this is trying to reach is for teenagers and young-adults.
  • Play Time: It takes about 2 minutes to clear all the ghosts, it can be longer if you spend more time dodging and conserving health.
